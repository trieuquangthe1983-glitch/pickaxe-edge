# Prospect research template

Fill this out per prospect BEFORE drafting a DM. The whole point is to make
your DM feel like a 1:1 message, not a blast. Spend 5 minutes per prospect.

## Copy-paste this block per prospect

```
Handle:            @
Real name:         
Primary platform:  (YouTube / Twitter / LinkedIn / Substack / TikTok / Reddit)
Follower count:    
Niche:             (crypto / longevity / B2B SaaS / home automation / AI eng / personal finance)
Best recent post:  (URL — the one with highest engagement in last 14 days)
What's their hook? (One sentence: how do they grab attention?)
Where are they NOT yet? (Which of the 6 platforms do they NOT publish to?)
Personalization hook: (One specific thing from their last 3 posts you can reference)
Best contact channel: (DM / email / contact form)
Status:            new / messaged / replied / booked / closed / dead
```

## Filling it out — example

```
Handle:            @cryptohayes
Real name:         Arthur Hayes
Primary platform:  Substack (Twitter secondary)
Follower count:    Substack ~50k, Twitter ~600k
Niche:             crypto_trading
Best recent post:  https://cryptohayes.substack.com/p/[example]
What's their hook? Macro framing + colorful storytelling on crypto derivatives
Where are they NOT yet? TikTok (zero), LinkedIn (zero), YouTube (negligible)
Personalization hook: Last essay opened with the Fed dot plot — I can show him
                     where else his audience is asking about that
Best contact channel: Twitter DM (open)
Status:            new
```

> Note: that's a high-ceiling prospect for illustration. Your real list should
> be **10k-100k followers** — they have cash but lack agency contracts.

## Red flags (skip the prospect)

- Posts only every 2+ weeks -> they're not in content-marketing mode
- Last 10 posts all promote one product -> they're a marketer, not a creator
- Replied to nobody in their last 50 mentions -> won't reply to you either
- Already publishes on all 6 platforms -> no arbitrage to sell them
- Sells "content strategy" themselves -> competitor

## Green flags (prioritize)

- Just hit a milestone (10k followers, $X MRR, launched a thing) -> open to conversation
- Recently complained about plateauing or running out of ideas
- Has a clear ONE platform dominance but 0 presence elsewhere
- Engages with replies in DMs (not just QTs)
- Recently asked their audience for tool/topic ideas
